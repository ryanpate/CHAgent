Key Differences

Conversation is Primary

Discussion is the main view, not buried under a "comments" toggle. You see the conversation the moment you open a task.

Decisions are Captured

Any comment can be marked as a "decision." Decisions are highlighted in-thread and collected in a project-level Decisions tab.

Unread Tracking

Per-user read state on every task thread. Badge counts show unread comments. @mentions trigger push notifications.
