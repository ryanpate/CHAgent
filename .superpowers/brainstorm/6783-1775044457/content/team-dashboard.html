Dashboard Features

  • Stats bar: Total, Pending, Approved, Not Added, Avg Rating
  • Insight cards: Top-rated pending song + most submitted artist

  • Filters: Status filter + sort (newest, oldest, highest rated, most votes)
  • Inline star rating: Click stars directly in the list — HTMX updates without page reload

  • Click row to open detail page for status changes and review notes
