Frequently asked questions

Are these worship team resources really free?

Yes. Every template and guide here is free to download and use for your church or worship ministry, with no account required. You only need an Aria account if you want to manage your team with the app itself.

What's the best way to organize a worship team schedule?

Start from a recurring rotation so no volunteer serves too often, track blockout dates before you schedule, and confirm each person before publishing. Our free worship schedule template gives you a ready-made structure for vocals, band, and tech, and Planning Center automates the same workflow once your team grows.

Do I need Planning Center to use these templates?

No. The volunteer application and schedule templates work as standalone spreadsheets and documents. The Planning Center setup guide is for teams that use — or want to start using — Planning Center Services to schedule people and plan songs.

How does Aria help beyond these templates?

Aria connects to your Planning Center data and answers questions like who's serving this Sunday, remembers volunteer prayer requests and follow-ups, and helps you care for your team instead of managing spreadsheets. See how AI works with Planning Center for a deeper look.
