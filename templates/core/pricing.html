Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.

What happens after my trial?

After your 14-day trial, you'll need to choose a paid plan to continue using Aria. Your data is preserved during this process.

Do I need a Planning Center account?

While Aria works best with Planning Center integration, you can use many features without it. PCO integration is optional.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards through Stripe. For Enterprise plans, we can arrange invoicing.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption, and your data is completely isolated from other organizations.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period.
